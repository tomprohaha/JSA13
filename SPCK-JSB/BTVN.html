LOCATION

Phú Quốc Island offers an unspoiled sanctuary of peace and tranquillity just 50 minutes by plane from Ho Chi Minh City.

Nestled on the sands of Phú Quôc’s stunning southwest coast and cocooned by lush gardens, La Veranda Resort Phú Quốc
transports you to a forgotten age of elegance.

Phú Quốc Island is 528 square kilometres of dense forest and palm-fringed beaches. Locals call it ‘The Island of 99
Mountains’, but you have only one name for it: paradise.
